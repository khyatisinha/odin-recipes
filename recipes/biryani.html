Biryani

This is an authentic Indian recipe.

Chicken biryani is a delicious Indian rice dish and involves length preparations.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Garam masala
  • Indian spices
  • 3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken pieces cut into chunks

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken.
  2. Boil the rice.Wash rice well and drain in a colander for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until golden. Add cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, ground ginger, and saffron
  5. Add marinated chicken to rice and let it cook for 30-40 minutes.
